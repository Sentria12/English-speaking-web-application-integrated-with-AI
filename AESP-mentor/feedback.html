Feedback Guidelines

Pronunciation Feedback

  • Identify specific sound errors
  • Demonstrate correct pronunciation
  • Suggest practice exercises
  • Use phonetic symbols when helpful
Example: "Try pronouncing 'th' with your tongue between teeth"

Grammar Feedback

  • Correct errors clearly
  • Explain the rule briefly
  • Provide correct examples
  • Suggest grammar resources
Example: "Use 'have been' for actions starting in past, continuing now"

Fluency Feedback

  • Note hesitation patterns
  • Suggest linking words
  • Encourage thought grouping
  • Recommend speaking pace
Example: "Try grouping words in phrases instead of single words"

Positive Reinforcement

  • Acknowledge improvements
  • Highlight strengths
  • Encourage continued practice
  • Be specific with praise
Example: "Your vocabulary has improved significantly this week!"
