Leveling Guidelines

Beginner

  • Basic vocabulary and simple sentences
  • Frequent pronunciation errors
  • Needs help with basic grammar
  • Limited conversational ability

Intermediate

  • Can handle daily conversations
  • Some pronunciation issues
  • Understands basic grammar rules
  • Needs practice with fluency

Advanced

  • Fluent in most situations
  • Minor pronunciation refinements needed
  • Focus on idiomatic expressions
  • Professional/business English
